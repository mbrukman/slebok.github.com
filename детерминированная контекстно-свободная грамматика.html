Deterministic context-free grammar (DCFG) is a context-free grammar that can be derived from a deterministic pushdown automaton (DPDA).

Translations

  • Abbreviated as: DCFG
  • English: deterministic context-free grammar (Wikipedia)
  • Russian: детерминированная контекстно-свободная грамматика

Publications

  • On Context-Free Languages and Push-Down Automata
  • Application of Pushdown-Store Machines
