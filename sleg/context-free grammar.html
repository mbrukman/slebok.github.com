English

  • context-free grammar (CFG)
  • a formal grammar in which every production rule has a form of n → w, where n is a single nonterminal symbol and w is a sequence of terminals and nonterminals.
  • Wikipedia: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Context-free_grammar
