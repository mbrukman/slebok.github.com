English

  • generalized multitext grammar (GMTG)
  • Generalized Multitext Grammar (GMTG) is a synchronous grammar formalism that is weakly equivalent to Linear Context-Free Rewriting Systems (LCFRS), but retains much of the notational and intuitive simplicity of Context-Free Grammar (CFG).
  • Publication: Generalized multitext grammars
